Research

My research focuses on understanding the processes that lead to the formation of clouds and precipitation. In particular, I am interested in the formation of ice in mixed-phase clouds. Currently, I am working on field and laboratory experiments in Colorado involving biological ice-nucleating particles. My research aims to better understand the atmosphere and hydrosphere and ultimately improve weather and climate predictions.
